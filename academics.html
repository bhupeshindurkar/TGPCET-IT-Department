Academic Programs

Industry-aligned curriculum designed to forge the next generation of digital architects

Vision & Mission

Vision

To emerge as a learning hub and center of excellence in the domain of Information Technology, empowering students with skills needed to lead in a global digital economy.

Mission

  • Impart quality technical education
  • Address societal and industry challenges
  • Foster research and innovation
  • Develop professional ethics

Program Outcomes (POs)

PO1: Engineering Knowledge

Apply knowledge of mathematics, science, engineering fundamentals, and IT specialization to solve complex engineering problems

PO2: Problem Analysis

Identify, formulate, research literature, and analyze complex engineering problems reaching substantiated conclusions using first principles

PO3: Design/Development of Solutions

Design solutions for complex problems and design system components that meet specified needs with appropriate consideration for public health, safety, and cultural, societal, and environmental considerations

PO4: Conduct Investigations

Use research-based knowledge and research methods including design of experiments, analysis and interpretation of data, and synthesis of information to provide valid conclusions

PO5: Modern Tool Usage

Create, select, and apply appropriate techniques, resources, and modern engineering and IT tools including prediction and modeling to complex engineering activities with an understanding of the limitations

PO6: The Engineer and Society

Apply reasoning informed by contextual knowledge to assess societal, health, safety, legal, and cultural issues and the consequent responsibilities relevant to professional engineering practice

PO7: Environment and Sustainability

Understand the impact of professional engineering solutions in societal and environmental contexts, and demonstrate knowledge of, and need for sustainable development

PO8: Ethics

Apply ethical principles and commit to professional ethics and responsibilities and norms of engineering practice

PO9: Individual and Team Work

Function effectively as an individual, and as a member or leader in diverse teams, and in multidisciplinary settings

PO10: Communication

Communicate effectively on complex engineering activities with the engineering community and with society at large, such as being able to comprehend and write effective reports and design documentation, make effective presentations, and give and receive clear instructions

PO11: Project Management

Demonstrate knowledge and understanding of engineering and management principles and apply these to one's own work, as a member and leader in a team, to manage projects and in multidisciplinary environments

PO12: Life-long Learning

Recognize the need for, and have the preparation and ability to engage in independent and life-long learning in the broadest context of technological change

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

PSO1

Software Development & Engineering

Ability to design, develop, test, and maintain robust software applications using modern programming languages, frameworks, and software engineering methodologies to solve real-world problems

PSO2

System Analysis & Design

Ability to analyze, design, implement, and manage complex IT systems and infrastructure solutions addressing organizational and societal needs with consideration for security, scalability, and performance

PSO3

Emerging Technologies

Ability to apply knowledge of emerging technologies including Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, IoT, Big Data Analytics, and Cybersecurity to develop innovative solutions for contemporary challenges

Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

PEO1: Professional Excellence

Graduates will excel in professional careers in Information Technology and related fields, demonstrating technical competence, leadership, and ethical practices

PEO2: Innovation & Research

Graduates will engage in research, innovation, and entrepreneurship to develop solutions for societal and industrial challenges

PEO3: Lifelong Learning

Graduates will pursue lifelong learning through higher education, professional development, and continuous skill enhancement to adapt to evolving technologies

PEO4: Social Responsibility

Graduates will demonstrate social responsibility, professional ethics, and contribute positively to society through their technical expertise
