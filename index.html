About the Department

The Department of Information Technology at Tulsiramji Gaikwad-Patil College of Engineering & Technology (TGPCET) was established in 2007 with an intake of 60 students. Over the years, we have grown into a premier institution for IT education in Nagpur, producing skilled professionals who excel in the global technology landscape. Our department is NBA accredited, ensuring world-class education standards and industry-relevant curriculum.

We focus on holistic development through a perfect blend of theoretical knowledge and practical skills. Our state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections provide students with the best learning environment. The department emphasizes innovation, research, and entrepreneurship, preparing students to become future leaders in technology.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To emerge as a learning hub and center of excellence in the domain of Information Technology.

Mission

  • To impart quality technical education through effective teaching learning process.
  • To provide a platform to address societal issues as well as challenges faced by IT industries.
  • To foster a culture of research and impart innovative and entrepreneurial skills in the field of IT.
  • To ensure overall development of students and staff by inculcating knowledge and professional ethics as a part of lifelong learning.
